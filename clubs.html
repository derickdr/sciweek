1. AMS Autism Associates Club

    Welcome! We are a group of students interested in learning more about Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) and other related developmental disorders. We engage in seminars, individual presentations, community events, and other learning opportunities to reach our goal! We also reach out to the community of families with children with ASD and bring fun interactive events for them! Come join!

  2. UBC PATHS

    UBC PATHS (Parkinson’s and Alzheimer’s Targeted Hope and Support) is a non-profit community-based club that focuses on raising awareness and funds for common neurodegenerative diseases. We engage the campus through our educational series, enrich our community through fundraisers for the Parkinson's and Alzheimer's Societies of BC, and explore with our students and academic professionals through our case studies and conference. Proceeds are directly donated to the Parkinson Society British Columbia and the Alzheimer Society British Columbia.

  3. Integrated Sciences Student Association

    Similar to the way that Integrated Science connects areas of science, UBC ISSA focuses on connecting people (especially ISCI students!) We work with the Integrated Sciences office and the Science Undergraduate Society (SUS) to provide the best academic, professional, and social opportunities to our ever-growing and educationally-diverse community. Visit our website to learn more about us and what we do!

  4. The UBC Astronomy Club

    "We are a group of students at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver who share a passion for astronomy. We welcome students of any discipline who would like to meet other people with like-minded interests. Let's explore the wonders of the universe together! Our club's goal is to educate and promote interest in astronomy through the various types of events we run, including: dark-sky observations, social activities, a lecture series, and outreach initiatives. We're always looking for more opportunities. The UBC Astronomy Club is committed to being an open and inclusive club for everyone regardless of race, religion, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, or disability."

  6. Biological Sciences Society (BIOSOC)

    "The UBC Biological Sciences Society (BIOSOC) is a student-run organization that has been continuously growing for decades. What started as a small group of students looking to hold social events has become one of the largest science societies on campus. We strive to cater to the needs and desires of Biology students, offering numerous involvement opportunities in research, academics, and recreation. Our goal is to build a vibrant, supportive community here in UBC Biology. Contact us on Facebook, Instagram, or CampusBase, or email us at biosoc@zoology.ubc.ca!"

  9. Stem Fellowship UBC

    STEM Fellowship is a student-run, national organization that provides a platform for students interested in STEM fields to connect with one another and with professionals, engage in research and improve skills in scholarly writing.   10. Friends of MSF (Doctors Without Borders) UBC [ FoMSF UBC]

    "FoMSF is a student support organization of Medecins Sans Frontieres. MSF (Doctors Without Borders) is a private international medical humanitarian non-profit organization that provides free healthcare in conflict zones and in countries affected by endemic diseases or natural disasters. We support MSF by raising funds to contribute to their work and by spreading awareness of the important work that MSF does around the world, on UBC campus and community. Join us in the live event to learn more about us!! "
